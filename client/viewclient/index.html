About SteamSal.es

SteamSal.es is a new way to browse the Steam Store.

Filter and sort by tag, discount, developer, or publisher. Or find top recommendations from our team. SteamSal.es has everything you'll need to find games you'll love.

Found a bug, have a suggestion, or just want to say hi?

Feel free to drop bug reports and suggestions on GitHub, or shoot an e-mail to developers at cutie dot cafe. We can't wait to hear from you!
